About

QUIRINO

Quirino is a landlocked province in the Philippines located in the Cagayan Valley Region in Luzon.
Its capital is Cabarroguis. Quirino, dubbed as the “Forest Heartland of Cagayan Valley”, was created in 1971
and named after former President Elpidio Quirino, the 6th President of the Philippines.
The province is known for its rugged topography, which makes it a beautiful paradise for outdoor-loving travelers.
